CINEMA

Our main activity is the screening of national and international films across various genres and formats. At UP, we aim to go beyond simply showing movies—after each screening, we hold discussions with the audience, and often host directors and/or filmmakers. For us, it is essential to promote dialogue and reflection around the film being presented.

This is why we place great importance on the curatorial work and careful selection of films carried out each month.

We have established partnerships with various players in the industry—such as production companies, distributors, and both public and private institutions—which have allowed us to host renowned film showcases and festivals, expanding the cultural offerings in this part of the city.
