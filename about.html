ABOUT UP

In a world where artistic expression is vital to cultural evolution, we are proud to present a unique space that merges different forms of creativity: cinema and visual art. Located on the outskirts of Mexico City, at UP we are dedicated to promoting the dissemination and production of art in all its forms.

We are more than a conventional cinema; we are a meeting point for cinephiles, artists, creators, and art lovers who seek to explore new perspectives and experiences. Our mission is to transcend boundaries and connect communities through the magic of cinema and the inspiration of visual art.

With a diverse program, we offer a carefully curated selection of films—primarily national cinema—ranging from classics to contemporary works, documentaries, and independent films that challenge, inspire, and provoke reflection.

In addition, our art gallery features rotating exhibitions by local and international artists, providing a platform for creative expression and cultural dialogue.
