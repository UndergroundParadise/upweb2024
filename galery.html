UP GALERY

Underground Paradise is also an art gallery—a space dedicated to promoting artistic production and dissemination on the outskirts of Mexico City. Here, we aim to break down barriers and push the boundaries of art, offering a place where both emerging and established artists can exhibit and share their work with the community. Our commitment is to foster cultural diversity and encourage creative dialogue, turning this space into a meeting point to appreciate, learn about, and enjoy art in all its forms.

exhibitions:

- THE HIDDEN MAGIC OF THE EVERYDAY (2024). Photography collective exhibition.
- PANORAMA (2022). Contemporary painting collective exhibition.
- RADAR ‘23 (2023). Contemporary printing techniques collective exhibition.

THE HIDDEN MAGIC OF THE EVERYDAY (2024)

This collective photography exhibition presents the sensitive and diverse perspectives of a group of Mexican photographers who, through their lenses, capture the richness of everyday life in various regions of the country. The exhibition offers a visual journey through urban and rural settings, celebrations, traditions, and landscapes that reveal the hidden magic of the everyday.

RADAR ‘23 (2023)

RADAR 23' was a printmaking exhibition featuring the work of 27 artists selected through an open call. The chosen works varied in format and technique, ranging from engraving, woodcut, and intaglio to digital printmaking and other graphic reproduction methods, including watercolor and illustration.

PANORAMA (2022)

PANORAMA was a contemporary painting exhibition curated by visual artist Erick Ruiz Lugo. The show featured 10 works in various formats and techniques, showcasing the current work of Mexican artists.
